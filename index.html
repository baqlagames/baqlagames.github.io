Welcome to Baqla

Step into a world where enchanted fruits battle for glory at the card table.

Welcome to the magical tavern where fruit creatures gather to play cards, wager treasures, and climb the ranks. Baqla is a multiplayer card game set in a whimsical fantasy world filled with enchanted arenas, secret power cards, and fierce competition.

Choose from 6 unique mini-games, each with its own strategy and arena. Play against friends in private rooms or challenge rivals on the weekly leaderboard. Customize your cards, unlock rare skins, and collect mystical rewards.

Whether you're matching fruit cards in the ancient ruins, racing to clear your hand in the dark caves, or stopping the timer at the perfect moment — every round is a new adventure.

6 Unique Game Modes

Each mode has its own enchanted arena, rules, and strategy. Master them all.

Full House

Be the first to collect 5 matching fruit cards. Grab cards, react fast, and use secret powers to steal from your rivals.

Flag Rush

Match the flag color and race to reach zero cards first. Use Change Color, Freeze, Draw 2, and Protection cards strategically.

Spin and Win

Spin the mystical reels and match symbols before time runs out. Use Slow Down and Freeze powers to gain the edge.

Tick Tock Boom

Stop the timer as close to the target as possible. Precision and nerves of steel win this mystical challenge.

Pattern

Memorize and repeat the pattern sequence. Test your focus in an enchanted underwater arena.

Memory Match

Find matching pairs of cards before your opponents. Speed and memory are your greatest weapons.

More Than Just Cards

A full game experience — compete, collect, customize, and conquer.

Competitive

Weekly Leaderboard

Climb the ranks and compete against players worldwide. Top players earn exclusive rewards every week. Track your score, check rival rankings, and claim your prizes before the timer resets.

Daily

Missions & Challenges

Complete daily challenges to earn coins and rare rewards. Each challenge tests a different skill — use secret cards, win specific modes, or achieve special objectives. New challenges every day.

Customize

Shop & Collection

Browse and collect stunning card skins, arena maps, hand gloves, and unique avatars. Each item transforms your gameplay experience with gorgeous hand-painted art.

Multiplayer

Play With Friends

Create private or public rooms and choose from all 6 game modes. Share your Room ID, invite friends, add bots, and compete head-to-head in enchanted arenas.

Rewards

Monthly Calendar

Log in daily to claim escalating rewards. Each day brings better prizes — coins, rare items, and exclusive unlocks. The longer your streak, the bigger the reward.

Personalize

13 Languages & Full Settings

Play in your language — Baqla supports 13 languages including Arabic, Japanese, Korean, and more. Customize your profile, display name, audio, and controls to your liking.
